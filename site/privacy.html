Privacy Policy

Wine Cellar Card is an offline-first app. All data you enter — including name, vintage, region, drinkByYear — is stored locally on your device using the app's local file storage and is never transmitted to any server. We do not collect, upload, sell, or share any personal data. The app makes no network requests except to Apple's App Store servers for in-app purchase verification and restore functionality, which is handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework.

If you delete the app, your local data is deleted with it. We have no way to access, view, or recover it.
